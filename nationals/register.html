Moonshine Park August 6th 2016

National Championship Terms

By submitting your details and payment you
  • Agree to comply with the Cyclocross National Championships Race Rules (which can be viewed here)
  • Acknowledge that you understand the risks associated with cyclocross racing and that you have a responsibility to look after your own safety (and the safety of others in your care) when competing and when at the event
  • Agree to allow the event organisers to use images of you for promotional purposes.

The elite races are for the overall men's and women's national champion titles. No licence is required to enter these races.

The age grade races are for men's and women's age grade national champion titles. Age grade races should be entered based on the age you will be at 31 December 2016.

Races will be electronically timed and timing chips must be returned at the end of the race. If your chip is not returned, you'll be charged $55 to replace them. We suggest just returning your chip.

You'll receive an email confirming your entry within 48 hours.
