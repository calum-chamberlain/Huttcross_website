National Championships 2016

Presented, with love, by Hüttcross.

Hüttcross is most pleased to bring New Zealand the 2016 Cyclocross National Championships on 6 August at Moonshine Park, Upper Hutt. Entries to the event are available to purchase between now and 31 July (right here).

The event will feature the following races:

  • Elite Men (60 minutes)
  • Elite Women (60 minutes)
  • Two age grade races (50 minutes)

None of these races require a license to enter, but you must agree to be bound by the race rules when entering (available to view here).

The races will be followed by a prizegiving/party in Upper Hutt, and the final round of the Hüttcross regular season the next day (where we'll be drawing our series spot prizes of a custom CX frame from Crucial Custom Cycles and a CX wheelset from Wheelworks. We expect it'll be rad.

We're also stoked to have the support of the Bluebridge Bike Club, who are offering half price flexi-fares to those travelling up from down South. A code for this discount will be sent along with your entry confirmation email.

More information about the event - including details about the venue, race schedule and party will be posted prior to the event.

If you've got any queries, feel free to flick us an email.

Otherwise - we look forward to proving Hüttcross-good-times to the the New Zealand cyclocross community!
