Pricing and entry

Hüttcross is a community driven race series, run for the people, by the people - we are in it for a good time, not to make money.

For 2018 regular series entry will be at $15 for online pre-entries, and $20 for on the day entries.

Please arrive in good time prior to your race, expect entry to take at least 15 minutes, and allow time to warm-up. Please bring cash! You will have to follow the rules.

For members and those who pre-enter online your registration on the day is a lot easier: we won't need you to fill out the whole form!

On the day registration opens at 9am and not before, and closes at 12pm. If you want to arrive earlier than 9am and help out, that would be most appreciated! We will be on-site from before 7am.

Race Entry

Online registration will be available from mid-May. The prices for the series will be:

Online entry fee per race (entries close midnight the night before): $15

On the day entry fee per race: $20 (cash only)

Online season entry (six races for the price of five): $75

As with previous years, the Kids' Grade will be free to enter.

Pre-entry for each race will close at midnight on the Thursday before each round
