Membership Terms

By becoming a member of Huttcross, you will be bound by constitution and the rules of the Club (which you can view here). Members 18 years of age and over can also stand as and vote for management committee candidates at the Annual General Meeting. Membership must be renewed annually, with the current membership year ending on 31 December.

By submitting your details you agree to become a member of the Huttcross Cyclocross Club Inc for the 2017 membership year. Membership will be confirmed by email once your payment has been received.
