Venues

Hüttcross 2016 Race Venues

Ngati Tama Park

A name change for 2016, this park first felt the caress of cross tyres in 2015 under the name Awa Kairangi. A large, curving stop-bank combines with a range of dry gravel soil and classic Hüttcross bog to give great variety.

Find your way here.

Moonshine Park

Moonshine is the classic Wellington cyclocross venue, combining a steep stop-bank for more off-camber than you could reasonably want, with flats, trees and bogs.

For 2016, Moonshine Park will host the NZ National Cyclocross Championships.
